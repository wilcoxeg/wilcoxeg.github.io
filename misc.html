Boston Brutalist Architecture Tour
I am a huge architecture fan, in general, but particularly fascinated by this idealistic yet flawed architectural style from the 1970s. Boston has some of the world's best brutalist architecture, so I put together a tour in the hopes of creating --- if not love --- than at least sympathy for these often-maligned buildings.
